That's my llama (2023)

It is the time of the year again! Every year, two teams in Llama Land will compete to see who can train all the llamas the fastest. Gather resources and use them wisely! Will team Llamanade or team Llamazing be the first to win?

  • Game design: Amaryllis studio
  • Art and graphic design: Amaryllis studio
  • Card count: 9
  • Number of players: 2
  • Age: 8+
  • Approximate playing time: 25-35 minutes
