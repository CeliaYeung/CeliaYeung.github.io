Cat cab (2022)

Welcome to Mew York City! The city is home to a 1000 little cats and is also one of the busiest city in the world! The Cat Cab Crew is recruitng new drivers for the city. You and two other little cats have signed up to be part of the crew, however only one of you may get the job. You have to prove that you are the most worthy recruit by collectng as many passengers as possible and taking them to their destinations swiftly!

  • Game design: Amaryllis studio
  • Art and graphic design: Amaryllis studio
  • Card count: 1
  • Number of players: 1-3
  • Age: 8+
  • Approximate playing time: 20-30 minutes
